Submission Guidelines

To be included in the Fanfiction Writers Collective:

  • You must write and publish fanfiction
  • It must be your personal site or blog (your fanfic can be on another domain/subdomain. I encourage you to also have your fanfiction backed up on your site!)
  • The site should reflect something about yourself
